Startup Cielo™

Commited to Startup Thriving™

Startup Cielo™ is a one-year comprehensive blended incubator for startups in pre-seed and seed stages of growth with risk-takers between 13 to 65+ years old. We combine hands-on experimentation with executive/team coaching and academic curriculum to help our participants bring their idea to life.
Our incubator utilizes a proprietary scientific framework as a backdrop for the method, the length, the depth, and the intensity of the programs, and is driven forward by the unique ability of our coaches and mentors to connect with the participants and their startups on a truly individual basis.
