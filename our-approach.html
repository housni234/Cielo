Startup Cielo™ Approach
Startup Cielo™ has a unique approach to startup development, backed up by proprietary frameworks and innovative research in the fields of entrepreneurship, organization structure, systematic venture development, and entrepreneurship education.
Our environment combines in-depth individual coaching, rigorous group mentoring, a diversified curriculum from a variety of business school subjects, and complete customization toward a participant's startup idea.
Startup Cielo™ approach is built on three main pillars of effectiveness: systematization,  individualization, and experimentation. All of our programs are offered virtually and last one calendar year from start to finish. Besides, they can be layered with an academic certificate degree from an accredited academic institution (at an additional cost paid directly to the business school).

Systematization

At the foundation of our startup incubator lies the logical math-inspired scientific framework, titled Organizational Coordinate System. This system is designed to provide business leaders with a set of questions and tools to design their organization and identify potential areas of resource and effort concentration. The organizational Coordinate System also allows the entrepreneurs a structured guided process for startup venture development. Every segment takes 2-3 months to complete and provide a segment outcome, which allows the startup program participant to move forward.

Individualization

In our startup incubator, we pay incredible attention to the concept of the individuality of a human. Our program is developed specifically for the underdogs, the individuals with limited resources, capacity, or other physical, mental, or emotional constraints, deemed unfit for success in the field of choice.
Our startup incubator program is designated to explore, challenge, and redefine an individual as a leader in their environment (incl. leadership values, style, strengths and weaknesses, areas for further development, and most effective communication approach), and redesigning the environment for the humanistic, sustainable, value-oriented outcome.
Our market includes:
  • aspiring entrepreneurs without an academic degree or extensive experience
  • experienced entrepreneurs with major fear block
  • business students, seeking applied learning
  • young executives with new projects
  • high school students with learning differences
  • student parents
  • retirees
  • post-military and post-incarceration trainees, reintegrating back into society
