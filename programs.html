Startup Cielo™ For Entrepreneurs
Startup Cielo for Entrepreneurs is a one-year virtual startup incubator, designed to help entrepreneurs give birth, nurture, and develop their business ideas into a self-sustaining startup. The program includes one-on-one coaching, group mentoring and coaching discussions, educational training, and can be layered with an academic certificate degree from selected accredited business schools (*at an additional cost paid directly to the institution). At the end of the year, the entrepreneurs will walk away with a living and breathing startup, prepared mentally, emotionally, and physically for rapid growth.
PRICE: €450 per month (12 months) / € 4950 per year
Prerequisite: Investor pitch deck, 2-min intro video, and an application.
Startup Cielo™ For Intrapreneurs
Startup Cielo for Intrapreneurs is a one-year virtual project incubator, designed to help young executives develop their project within a larger organization into a self-sustaining division or department. The program includes one-on-one coaching, group mentoring and coaching discussions, educational training, and can be layered with an academic certificate degree from selected accredited business schools (*at an additional cost paid directly to the institution of choice). At the end of the year, the intrapreneurs will walk away with a well-functioning entity within an entity, prepared mentally, emotionally and physically to lead it to long-term success.
PRICE: €625 per month (12 months) / € 7450 per year
Prerequisite: Project pitch deck, 2-min intro video, and an application.
Startup Cielo™ For Highschoolers
Startup Cielo for High Schoolers is a one-year extracurricular educational program, designed to educate teenagers on the basics of entrepreneurship, entrepreneurial mindset, and venture building while helping them to explore and develop their idea.
The program utilizes a customized and simplified business school baccalaureate curriculum and is suitable for a 1-1.5 credit equivalent for the homeschooling programs(USA). At the end of the year, the students will be able to provide an executive summary, investor pitch deck, and potentially have a side business for pocket money.
PRICE: €625 per month (12 months) / € 7450 per year
Prerequisite: Project pitch deck, 2-min intro video, and an application.
Startup Cielo™ For Tech Students
Startup Cielo for Tech Students is a one-year, virtual, unpaid internship program, designed to provide students in the technology sector with hands-on experience, working on real startup tech projects with a potential for hiring upon completion. In addition, our participants will be exposed to a general Startup Cielo™ program content, one-on-one coaching, group mentoring and coaching discussions, educational trainings, and can layer it with an academic certificate degree from selected accredited business schools (*at an additional cost paid directly to the institution of choice). 
PRICE: €0
Prerequisite: Cover letter, CV, and tech project portfolio (if available).
